About Me

Hi! I'm a senior at Stanford studying computer science, and I'm passionate about AR/VR and technologies that have positive impact in fields like health and fitness. My past work experience includes working as a software engineer in the Stanford Medical School as well as internships at Apple, Microsoft, Vanguard, and Yext.

Above, you can check out some of my personal projects. I invented a medical device that monitors physical therapy for the knee, and a patent was issued in 2022. I was also a founder at Queer Chart, an online platform that connected queer women at Stanford, and a co-founder of Sesame, a video calling platorm that allowed friends to replicate dorm hangouts during the COVID pandemic.

In my free time, I enjoy going for runs and playing chess.
